About Our Church

To God Be The Glory Full Gospel Church and Ministries is a Christ-centered community dedicated to worship, discipleship, and service. We exist to glorify God, share His love, and help people grow in faith.

Learn More

Our Founding

Established in faith and prayer, To God Be The Glory Church began as a small fellowship with a big vision: to reach our community with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Since 1979, we have grown into a vibrant church family, serving Banaba San Pascual Batangas and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples, loving our neighbors, and serving with compassion. We envision a church where every member is equipped to live out their faith, impact the world, and experience the transforming power of Christ.

Core Values:

  • Christ-centered Worship
  • Biblical Teaching
  • Community Outreach
  • Prayer & Fellowship
  • Family & Youth Development
